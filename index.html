About

As students navigate their academic journey, they often face challenges in efficiently accessing study resources and spaces. This can lead to wasted time and reduced productivity. Books&Study aims to solve this issue by offering an interactive, user-friendly platform designed to streamline the process of managing study-related needs.

  • Room Booking System: Users can reserve study rooms with real-time availability updates, ensuring they always have a space to focus.
  • Comprehensive Resource Search: A streamlined search tool to access books and academic materials quickly and efficiently.
  • Interactive User Interface: Designed to enhance usability, the interface ensures tasks can be completed with ease and minimal effort.

Our platform provides users with tools to book study rooms, search for books, and access curated academic resources. With an intuitive interface and real-time availability updates, Books&Study allows users to focus on their academic goals without unnecessary distractions. By integrating features that are accessible and easy to navigate, we cater to students, educators, and researchers alike.

Persona Type: 3rd Year College Student

Name: Rachel

Age: 20

Location: Salem, Massachusetts

Technical Comfort: Very Technically Savvy

Profession: Student/Part-time Software Engineer

Back Story:

  • Rachel has lived in Salem all her life but has recently moved on her own in a studio apartment.
  • She attends Boston University and has to take about an hour commute to school.
  • She is a Computer Science major and loves to draw in her free time.
  • Rachel has two best friends that she met in college and plans to create a startup with them.

Motivations:

  • Rachel wants to go to a quiet place to study for her midterms.
  • She is looking forward to visiting this library due to its aesthetics which helps her feel inspired to focus better.

Frustrations:

  • Rachel doesn't enjoy busy places.
  • When libraries don’t have many windows, it can cause people to feel trapped and make studying much more tiresome.

Ideal Experience

  • Rachel likes getting things done and feeling productive.
  • Rachel also loves Salem’s scenery and would like to have a nice view.
  • Since Rachel loves art, she pays close attention to details and loves the small things.
